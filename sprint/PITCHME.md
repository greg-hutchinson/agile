## Sprint Rules
- No “non-delivery team” changes
- Consistent sprint lengths
- Team chooses how to complete stories (by priority)

---
## Why such a short timeframe?
- Highest priority (ROI)
- Reduce risk
  - Risk Grows Exponentially
