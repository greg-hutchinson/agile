# Marshmallow Challenge
---

### Build the tallest freestanding structure with only the following supplies:
- 1 Meter of String
- 1 Meter of Tape
- 20 Pieces of Spaghetti
- 1 Marshmallow

And the marshmallow must be on top of the structure.
